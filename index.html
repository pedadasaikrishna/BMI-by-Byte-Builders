Diet Plans by BMI

  • Underweight: High-calorie diet with healthy fats, nuts, eggs, and dairy.
  • Normal: Balanced diet with grains, lean meats, and veggies.
  • Overweight: Fiber-rich and low-carb meals. Avoid sugar & saturated fats.
  • Obese: Very low-sugar, calorie deficit, high-protein, and doctor consultation.
